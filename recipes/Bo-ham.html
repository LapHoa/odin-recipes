Beef Staw instant pot

Description

Delicious and easy to make beef stew using instant pot

Ingredients

  • Beef shank
  • Carrot
  • Potato
  • Chinese 5-spice
  • Ginger, shallot, garlic

Steps

  1. Use saute mode of instant pot to saute ginger, shallot, garlic
  2. Put beef and spice in
  3. Set Instant pot to manual in 25 minutes
  4. Peel Carrot and Potatoes, cut into bite size
  5. When instant pot finish pressure cooking, vent it then put carrot and Potatoes into pot
  6. Change pot to saute, continue to cook for 15 minutes
