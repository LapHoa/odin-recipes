4-ingredient Milk Pudding

Description

Milk pudding is a fun and delicious dessert at home! Milk pudding is super rich and soft; one bite and it melts into your mouth. You can also swap for coconut milk if you want a lactose-free alternative

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Corn starch
  • Sugar
  • Shredded coconut

Steps

  1. Heat up one half of the milk
  2. Put in sugar and mix when milk gets warm
  3. Mix the other half with cornstarch
  4. Slowly pour it into the pot and keep stirring until the milk turn into a thick consistency
  5. Pour it into a container and let it rest till cool down
  6. Cut to bite-size and coat them with shredded coconut
